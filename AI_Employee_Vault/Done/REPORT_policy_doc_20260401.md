## 2. High-Risk Leave Days — Monday & Friday

> **2x salary deduction** for any leave taken on Monday or Friday.

This is a significant financial penalty. Employees should treat Monday/Friday leave requests as last resort and always provide documented proof.

**Sandwich Rule:** If leave is taken on both the day before AND after a weekend, the weekend day itself also becomes unpaid leave. Example: Leave on Friday + Monday = Saturday/Sunday also deducted.
